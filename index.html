GAME 11 tournament

Game 11 is a sports-based charity football competition and festival founded in 2017 by a group of entertainment committee members in Mfantsipim School. Initially executed in 2017 and commercialized in 2023, Game 11 offers a platform for young, gifted athletes to showcase their talents. Our mission is to reignite the passion for football in Ghana, develop talent, and enable underprivileged youth to be discovered by scouts.

ABOUT GAME 11

History and Background

Founded in 2017 by Mfantsipim’s entertainment committee members.

Mission Statement

To develop talent, promote football, and support charitable causes.

Achievements

Four successful tournaments, proceeds donated to charity.

Community Impact

Engaged over 2,000 participants and supported multiple charities.
